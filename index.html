About The Hackathon

Aviothic is the annual hackathon organized by CyberSristhi, Kanpur Institute Of Technology. It is an in-person 12-hr event for innovatively fostering real-world problem-solving skills.

  • Date: 29th May to 29th May 2023
  • Team Size: 1 to 4 members
  • Free registrations
  • Participation fee of Rs. 100 to be paid after getting shortlisted
  • Cash prizes will be announced soon!

FAQs

Answers to the most frequently asked questions!

Who can participate?

The hackathon is open for Students from all over India. It is the right place for anyone who's interested in learning and innovating with their ideas.

01

How much will it cost?

Although registration for Aviothic 1.0 is completely free, a small participation fee (100 INR per team) has to be paid incase your team is shortlisted.

02

How can I apply?

The registration would be simply done by just clicking the register button here and filling up a google form in which you would have to mention you and your team details. Registrations end on 25th May 2023

03

What if I don't know how to code?

No worries, Interest in learning and working with technology is much more important than your current experience level.

04

What are the prizes to be won?

We are going to announce some really exciting prizes and incentives soon. Stay tuned for further updates!

05

Can we apply as a team?

Yes! We believe that you’re stronger as a team than apart. You can form teams of 2-4 people. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and development skills.
